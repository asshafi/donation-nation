why is giving to charity so gratifying? We’ve taken a closer look at

Shelter for a family

Sharing the experience of donating to charity with your children shows them from a young age that they can make positive changes in the world.

Food for a family

Children naturally love to help others, so nurturing their innate generosity is likely to mean that they grow up with a greater appreciation of what they have, and will carry on supporting charity in years to come.

Education for a family

Starting a tradition of donating to charity with your children is easy - try creating a family donation box that everyone can add to and nominate a family charity each year, involving the children in choosing which causes to support.

Your Donation is others inspiration
